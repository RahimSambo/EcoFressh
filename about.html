Our Story

EcoFresh was founded in 2020 by environmentally-conscious individuals tired of cleaning products filled with harsh chemicals. What started as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a mission to revolutionize home cleaning.

Our Mission

To provide effective, sustainable cleaning solutions that protect both human health and the environment.

Our Values

Sustainability: We prioritize renewable resources and minimize our environmental footprint.

Transparency: We list every ingredient and are honest about our sourcing.

Community: We believe in building a community of eco-conscious individuals.
